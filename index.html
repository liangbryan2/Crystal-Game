Crystals Collector!

You are given a random Goal at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total Score.

You win the game by matching your total Score with the Goal. If your total Score goes above the Goal, you lose

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

Each time you start a new game, the vlue of the crystal is randomized.
